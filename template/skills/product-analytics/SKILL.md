# Skill: Product Analytics

Use when a change needs measurable product impact or behavioral insight.

## When to use

- Event tracking or funnel analysis.
- Feature experiments or A/B tests.
- Product KPI instrumentation.
- Understanding user behavior changes.

## Checklist

- Define the event, property, and goal.
- Tie each metric to a hypothesis.
- Avoid unnecessary or noisy events.
- Consider privacy and retention.
- Plan analysis before implementation.

## Guardrails

- Do not collect data without a purpose.
- Do not duplicate events across layers.
- Do not ignore consent or retention constraints.
