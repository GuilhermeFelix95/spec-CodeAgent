# Skill: Change Communication

Use when you need to explain a change clearly to humans who will review, operate, or depend on it.

## When to use

- PR descriptions or release notes.
- Handoffs between contributors.
- Risk and rollout summaries.
- Explaining trade-offs or follow-up tasks.

## Checklist

- Say what changed in plain language.
- Call out risk, impact, and rollback.
- Separate facts from assumptions.
- Keep the message short and action-oriented.
- Include the next step when relevant.

## Guardrails

- Do not bury risk in vague language.
- Do not overexplain unimportant details.
- Do not omit operational follow-up when it matters.
